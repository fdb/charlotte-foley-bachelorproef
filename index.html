" If you knew
how much i love you,
you'd cry for joy "

by Charlotte Foley

Living with two people that are merged into one.
You feel like you are losing one of the two.
You feel them slipping away from you.

You are grieving for someone who is still alive.

Every moment when they are normal, themselves;

you forget,

you forgive,

and you pretend.

You press down your feelings,
you put them away,
and try to forget about them.

Always be ready

to be let down.

to be disappointed.
